Your monthly subscription fee covers all the costs of your new developer. For a single flat fee, we’ll cover local payroll, benefits, taxes and compliance costs. You’ll never be asked to pay additional fees or expenses on top.

We accept all major card providers, and we charge in US dollars, regardless of where your developer is based. There’s no need to set up bank transfers, convert currency, or manage invoices.

Yes, if your needs change or you realize you need a more (or less!) experienced developer than you expected, you can change your subscription. We’ll adjust your fee before your next monthly payment.
